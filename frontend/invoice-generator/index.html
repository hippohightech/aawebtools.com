How this Invoice Generator actually works

This is a browser-first tool, not a SaaS. When you fill in the form and click Download PDF, the entire invoice is assembled, rendered, and saved on your own device using jsPDF. There is no POST request to our server that carries your business information — you can verify this in your browser's network tab.

That architecture drives three consequences worth knowing before you rely on the tool:

  • Privacy by design. Your client list, line items, and totals are never transmitted, logged, or readable by us. The trade-off: we cannot help you recover a lost invoice, because we never had it.
  • Works offline after first load. Once the page is loaded, you can disconnect your internet and the tool still functions. The PDF still downloads.
  • No server-side state. Closing the tab discards everything. For recurring billing or a persistent client database, use Zoho Invoice or Wave instead — see the comparison below.

When this tool is the right fit — and when it isn't

The most useful thing we can tell you up front is where this tool is weak, so you can pick the right one for your need.

Use AAWebTools Invoice Generator when you…

  • Are a freelancer, sole proprietor, or contractor issuing one-off or occasional invoices.
  • Bill in multiple currencies or in bilingual (EN/FR) contexts — for example, a Canadian freelancer with both US and Quebec clients.
  • Care about data privacy and prefer a tool that never sees your clients' names.
  • Need a printable PDF today, not an account to sign up for.

Use a different tool when you…

  • Need recurring or subscription billing. Use Zoho Invoice or Stripe Invoicing.
  • Want a client portal where customers log in to view and pay.
  • Must collect payment through the invoice itself (card, ACH). Use Wave or Stripe.
  • Are subject to EU B2B e-invoicing under the Peppol mandate (eInvoicing Directive 2014/55/EU). You need a certified Peppol access point, not a PDF tool.
  • Need team access or multi-user workflows.

What professional invoices must include — by jurisdiction

Mandatory invoice content varies by country. Here is the core of what the main English-speaking tax authorities require as of 2026. All of these fields are supported by the form above.

  • Canada (CRA). If you are GST/HST-registered, your invoice must show your 9-digit Business Number, the date, the buyer's name, a description of the goods/services, and the amount of GST/HST charged separately. Full rules: CRA invoice requirements. Quebec has additional TVQ number requirements (Revenu Québec).
  • United Kingdom (HMRC). VAT-registered businesses must show: invoice number (sequential), date of supply, supplier VAT number, customer name and address, description and quantity of goods/services, rate of VAT applied, and amount in pounds. Full rules: gov.uk VAT invoices.
  • European Union. VAT invoices must include supplier VAT ID, customer VAT ID for B2B, the nature of the supply, the tax rate, and the amount payable — with specific rules per Member State. B2B e-invoicing is increasingly mandatory under the Peppol framework.
  • United States. No federal invoice-content rule applies to non-VAT businesses, but all invoices should include a unique number, date, seller and buyer identification, a clear description, and any applicable state or local sales tax. If you receive 1099-NEC or 1099-MISC income, retain invoices for IRS audit purposes (Publication 583).
  • Australia (ATO). Tax invoices for sales over A$82.50 must show supplier identity, ABN, GST amount, and a clear "Tax Invoice" label.

Common mistakes we see: forgetting to show the VAT/GST registration number when registered, using non-sequential invoice numbering (a red flag for auditors), and omitting the due date.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Invoice Generator completely free — no watermark or hidden limit?

Yes. There is no watermark, no signup, no email collection, and no cap on how many invoices you can generate. The tool is free forever and is supported by non-intrusive advertising on the page.

Will the invoice be accepted by my clients and by tax authorities?

The generated PDF contains every field a standard commercial invoice requires (invoice number, date, billing/supplier details, line items, tax, total, payment terms). Formal legal validity depends on your jurisdiction — for example, Canada's CRA and the UK's HMRC have specific VAT/GST-registration-number requirements if you are registered. Enter those numbers in the appropriate fields and the invoice will meet the mandatory-content rules in most countries. For complex VAT situations or EU B2B e-invoicing under the Peppol mandate, use a compliance-certified service.

Which currencies and tax formats are supported?

Currency is a free-text field — you can enter any symbol ($, €, £, ¥, ₹) or ISO code. EN mode defaults to $, FR mode defaults to €. Tax lines are also free-text and support multiple lines per invoice — common labels GST, HST, PST, TPS, TVQ, TVA, VAT are pre-populated, but you can enter any label and any percentage. This works for Canadian provincial variants (including Quebec TVQ), EU VAT (rates 17–27%), UK VAT (20%), Australian GST (10%), US state sales tax, or any custom jurisdiction.

Can I save an invoice and edit it later?

No — this tool is deliberately stateless. Once you close or refresh the tab, the data is gone. This is a privacy trade-off: because nothing is stored on our servers, there is also nothing to re-open. If you need persistent invoice management, recurring billing, or a client database, use Zoho Invoice or Wave. For one-off or occasional invoicing, this tool is faster.

Can I add my company logo to the invoice?

Yes. Use the Business Logo upload field to attach any PNG, JPG, or SVG image. The logo is read into your browser, embedded as a base64 data URL in the PDF, and never transmitted to any server. It appears in the top-right of the generated invoice.

What data does this tool collect about me?

None of the invoice data reaches our servers. The PDF is generated client-side using jsPDF — there is no POST endpoint that would receive your business information, client details, or line items. We collect anonymous, cookie-free page-view analytics via Umami (browser, country, referrer) for traffic measurement only. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
