Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

AAWebTools ("we", "us", "our") operates aawebtools.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our free online tools.

1. Information We Collect

Data you provide: When you use our tools, you may enter URLs (for downloaders), text (for AI tools), or business/payment information (for invoice and pay stub generators). This data is processed to deliver the tool's functionality and is not stored on our servers unless explicitly stated below.

Automatically collected data: We use Umami Analytics (a privacy-focused, cookie-free analytics platform) to collect anonymous usage statistics including:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Browser type and screen size
  • Country (from anonymized IP)
  • Referral source

We do not collect names, email addresses, or personal identifiers through analytics.

2. How Each Tool Handles Your Data

TikTok Downloader & Twitter Downloader: URLs you submit are sent to our server to fetch the media. We do not log or store the URLs or downloaded content.

Invoice Generator & Pay Stub Generator: All data stays in your browser. PDFs are generated client-side using jsPDF. No business details, financial information, or employee data is ever sent to our servers.

AI Content Detector: Text analysis runs entirely in your browser. No text is sent to any server.

AI Text Humanizer: Text you submit is sent to a third-party AI API (Google Gemini) for rewriting. We do not store the input or output text. Google's privacy policy applies to their processing of this data.

3. Cookies

AAWebTools does not use cookies for tracking. Our analytics platform (Umami) is cookie-free and GDPR-compliant by design. Third-party advertising partners (PropellerAds, Google AdSense) may set cookies on downloader and tool pages to serve relevant ads. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Umami Analytics — privacy-focused, anonymous usage statistics
  • Google Gemini API — powers the AI Text Humanizer
  • PropellerAds — advertising on downloader pages
  • Google AdSense — advertising on generator and AI tool pages
  • Google Fonts — web font delivery (Inter)

Each service has its own privacy policy governing data they collect.

5. Data Retention

We do not store personal data. Analytics data is aggregated and anonymous. Server logs (IP addresses, request paths) are retained for up to 30 days for security monitoring and automatically deleted.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data or require accounts, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. If you believe we hold any of your data, contact us and we will address your request promptly.

7. Children's Privacy

Our tools are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Security

We protect our infrastructure with SSL/TLS encryption, rate limiting, firewall rules, and fail2ban intrusion prevention. All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our tools after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
