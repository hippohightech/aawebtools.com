Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

AAWebTools ("we", "us", "our") is operated by ScopeCove, based in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use aawebtools.com. It is written to meet the substantive requirements of Canada's PIPEDA, the EU GDPR (Article 13 notice), the UK GDPR, and the CCPA.

1. Information We Collect

Data you provide: When you use our tools, you may enter text (for AI tools) or business/payment information (for invoice and pay stub generators). With the single exception noted in Section 2 (AI Humanizer → Google Gemini), this data is processed entirely in your browser and is never transmitted to our servers.

Automatically collected data: We use Umami Analytics (privacy-focused, cookie-free) to collect anonymous usage statistics including pages visited, time on page, browser type, screen size, country (from anonymized IP), and referral source. We do not collect names, email addresses, or personal identifiers through analytics.

Server logs: Our web server retains standard access logs (IP address, request path, user agent, timestamp) for up to 30 days for security monitoring and abuse prevention. These logs are automatically purged after that window.

2. How Each Tool Handles Your Data

Invoice Generator & Pay Stub Generator: All data stays in your browser. PDFs are generated client-side using jsPDF. No business details, financial information, or employee data is ever sent to our servers. Clearing your browser's local storage removes any saved drafts.

AI Content Detector: Text analysis runs entirely in your browser. No text is sent to any server.

AI Text Humanizer — Third-party AI processing: Text you submit to the Humanizer is transmitted over HTTPS to Google's Gemini API (operated by Google LLC, United States) for the sole purpose of generating rewritten text, which is returned to your browser and discarded on our side — we do not log or persist the input, the output, or the association between the two.

  • Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)/(f)): performance of your requested service and our legitimate interest in delivering the tool.
  • Data transferred: the plain text you enter (up to 1,000 characters per request), plus a system prompt from us.
  • Recipient: Google LLC (Gemini API). Google's processing is governed by Google's Gemini API Additional Terms and Google's Privacy Policy.
  • International transfer: data may be processed in the United States. For EU/UK users, this transfer relies on the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework and Standard Contractual Clauses as applicable.
  • Retention on our side: none. The text is not written to our database or disk.
  • Right to decline: if you do not wish your text to be processed by Google, do not use the AI Text Humanizer tool. The other tools on AAWebTools do not send data to any third party.

3. Cookies

AAWebTools itself does not set tracking cookies. Our analytics platform (Umami) is cookie-free. Google AdSense, when an ad is served, may set advertising cookies for ad personalization and measurement. You can opt out of personalized advertising via Google's Ad Settings or via your browser's cookie controls.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Umami Analytics — cookie-free, anonymized usage statistics (self-hosted).
  • Google Gemini API — powers the AI Text Humanizer only. See Section 2 for the full disclosure.
  • Google AdSense — advertising on tool and blog pages.
  • Cloudflare — DNS, CDN, and DDoS protection.
  • Google Fonts — web font delivery.

Each service operates under its own privacy policy governing the data it processes on its infrastructure.

5. Data Retention

We do not store personal data. Analytics data is aggregated and anonymous. Server logs (IP addresses, request paths) are retained for up to 30 days for security monitoring and automatically deleted.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not create user accounts and do not retain the content you enter into our tools, there is typically no personal data for us to access, modify, or delete. Regardless, under PIPEDA, GDPR, UK GDPR, and CCPA you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction / Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — request deletion of any personal data we hold.
  • Objection / Opt-out — object to processing or opt out of sale/sharing (we do not sell or share personal data).
  • Data portability — receive a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Complaint — lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority (in Canada, the Office of the Privacy Commissioner; in the EU, your national DPA; in the UK, the ICO).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the contact page or scopecove.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Our tools are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Security

We protect our infrastructure with SSL/TLS encryption, rate limiting, firewall rules, and fail2ban intrusion prevention. All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our tools after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
