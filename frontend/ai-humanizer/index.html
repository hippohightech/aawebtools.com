FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AI text humanizer?

An AI text humanizer rewrites AI-generated content to sound more natural and human-written. It uses techniques from natural language generation to vary sentence length, add natural imperfections, use contractions, and adjust tone — making the text indistinguishable from human writing while preserving the original meaning.

Will humanized text pass AI detectors?

Our humanizer significantly reduces AI detection scores by introducing natural writing patterns. While no tool guarantees 100% undetectability, most users see their text score drop from 90%+ AI to under 30%. For writing tips, see the Grammarly Blog. Use our AI Detector tool to verify the results.

How many characters can I humanize for free?

You can humanize up to 1,000 characters per request, completely free. There's no daily limit on the number of requests. This is enough for most paragraphs, email drafts, and social media posts.

Which AI tools does this work with?

Our humanizer works with text from any AI writing tool: ChatGPT (GPT-4, GPT-3.5), Claude, Gemini, Llama, Jasper, Copy.ai, and all others. Simply paste the AI-generated text and click Humanize.

Is this free to use?

Yes, completely free. No signup, no account, no credit card. We use Claude AI to rewrite your text and support this tool through non-intrusive advertising. Your text is processed securely and never stored.
