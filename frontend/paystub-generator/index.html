FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a pay stub generator?

A pay stub generator creates professional payroll documents showing an employee's earnings, deductions, and net pay. Our tool supports 9 countries with country-specific deductions (FICA, CPP, NIC, etc.), multiple templates, and instant PDF download — all free, no signup. For US payroll tax details, see the IRS employment tax guide.

Does this support CPP and EI for Canada?

Yes! Select "Canada" or "Canada (Quebec)" and CPP/QPP, EI, and all provincial deductions are automatically added. Quebec includes QPP, QPP2, QPIP, and reduced EI rate. All rates are pre-filled and editable. See the CRA payroll page for current rates.

Is there a French bulletin de paie generator for Quebec?

Yes! Click the FR toggle to switch all labels to French. Select "Canada (Quebec)" for Quebec-specific deductions (RRQ, RQAP, RRQ2). The PDF output uses proper Canadian French terminology: Bulletin de paie, Retenues, Salaire net, etc.

Can I create a UK payslip with NIC and pension?

Yes. Select "UK" and the deductions automatically include Income Tax PAYE, National Insurance (NIC at 8%), Workplace Pension (5%), and optional Student Loan repayment. The UK template also shows Total Hours Worked as legally required.

Is this free with no sign up required?

100% free, forever. No signup, no watermark, no limits. Your data stays in your browser — nothing is sent to any server. Works on all devices.
