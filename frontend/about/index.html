About AAWebTools

Last updated: April 17, 2026

What AAWebTools Is

AAWebTools is a suite of free, browser-first online tools built and maintained by ScopeCove. Our focus is a small number of tools done thoroughly rather than a large catalogue done poorly. Every active tool is listed below, with the methodology and data-handling notes that apply to it.

Active Tools

  • Invoice Generator — Create PDF invoices in the browser. No account, no upload. Supports USD, CAD, EUR, GBP, and multi-region tax labels.
  • Pay Stub Generator — Generate country-specific pay-stub templates (USA, Canada, UK, France, Germany, Australia, International) as a PDF, client-side. Informational and novelty use only — see our Terms of Service.
  • AI Content Detector — Heuristic estimator for AI-generated text likelihood. Statistical signal, not a legal or academic verdict.
  • AI Text Humanizer — Rewrites AI drafts for style and readability using Google Gemini. Intended for business writing, not academic submission — see our Terms of Service.

Our Methodology

Every tool on this site follows the same build standard, documented here so users can judge results with context:

  • Browser-first where possible. Invoice Generator, Pay Stub Generator, and AI Detector run entirely in-browser — no data leaves your device.
  • Third-party APIs disclosed. When a tool must call an external API (AI Humanizer uses Google Gemini), we say so explicitly on the tool page and in our Privacy Policy.
  • Honest limits over marketing. Every tool page has a "When NOT to use this" section. We would rather lose a user than give them a result that misleads them.
  • Updates are dated. Each tool shows a "last reviewed" date. Major tax, payroll, or API changes trigger a documented review.

Editorial Policy

Content on AAWebTools — including blog posts, tool explanations, and comparison tables — is written or reviewed by the ScopeCove team and is intended to be factually accurate and genuinely useful. We follow three rules:

  • Cite primary sources. When we quote tax rules, detector methodology, or payroll rates, we link the authoritative source (IRS, HMRC, Revenu Québec, OpenAI policy docs, etc.), not secondary blogs.
  • Comparison tables are honest. If a competitor is better on a specific axis, we say so — and we include a "When NOT to use AAWebTools" row on comparison tables as a matter of course.
  • Corrections are public. Material errors are corrected in place with a dated note at the bottom of the page. We do not silently rewrite history.

If you find a factual error, tax rule out of date, or misleading statement, please contact us — we will review and correct within 48 hours or explain why we believe the original is accurate.

Data Handling & Privacy

Our Privacy Policy is the authoritative document. Summary:

  • Invoice Generator, Pay Stub Generator, AI Detector — entirely client-side; your data never reaches our servers.
  • AI Humanizer — text is sent to Google's Gemini API for rewriting. We do not log or store the input or output. Google's own Gemini API policy governs their side.
  • Analytics — we use Umami, a privacy-focused, cookie-free analytics platform. No names, emails, or personal identifiers are collected.
