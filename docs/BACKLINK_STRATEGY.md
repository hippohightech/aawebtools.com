## What To AVOID (Will Get Penalized)

1. Do NOT buy backlinks from Fiverr
2. Do NOT use automated link generator tools
3. Do NOT use exact-match anchor text >10% of links
4. Do NOT submit to 100 directories in one day
5. Do NOT publish same article on multiple platforms
6. Do NOT spam Reddit/HN with self-promotion
7. Do NOT participate in link exchanges
8. Do NOT create thin 300-word Web 2.0 posts
